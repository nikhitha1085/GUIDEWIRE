"# GUIDEWIRE" 
# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Delivery Workers

## Problem Statement
Gig delivery workers from platforms like Swiggy, Zomato, Zepto and Amazon depend on daily deliveries for their income. However external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, pollution or local restrictions reduce their working hours and cause significant income loss.

Currently most gig workers do not have financial protection against such disruptions. When these events occur they lose a large portion of their daily earnings.

This project proposes an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that automatically compensates delivery workers when external disruptions reduce their ability to work.

---

## Target Persona

Name: Ramesh Kumar  
Age: 28  
Location: Hyderabad  
Occupation: Food Delivery Partner (Swiggy/Zomato)

Ramesh works around 9–10 hours per day and earns ₹700–₹1000 daily depending on deliveries. During heavy rain or pollution his working hours reduce significantly which causes a loss of ₹300–₹500 per day.

The system will help protect workers like Ramesh from income loss.

---

## Proposed Solution

The platform provides AI-powered parametric insurance for gig delivery workers. It continuously monitors weather and environmental data. When predefined conditions such as heavy rainfall or high pollution levels occur the system automatically triggers a claim and provides compensation.

This ensures fast and automated financial protection for workers.

---

## System Workflow

1. Worker registers on the platform
2. AI calculates weekly insurance premium
3. System monitors external disruptions
4. If disruption occurs claim is automatically triggered
5. Worker receives instant payout

---

## Weekly Premium Model

Low Risk: ₹10 per week → ₹500 coverage  
Medium Risk: ₹20 per week → ₹700 coverage  
High Risk: ₹30 per week → ₹1000 coverage

Premium is calculated based on location and historical risk data.
